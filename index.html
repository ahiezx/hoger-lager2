Game Rules:

  1. The computer will generate a random number between 1 and 100
  2. The player will guess if the next number will be higher or lower
  3. If the player is correct, they win 5 points
  4. If the player is incorrect, they lose 3 points
  5. The game ends when the player has 0 points
  6. The player can reset the game at any time


Bonus:

  1. Increase the difficulty of the game after the player reaches 100 points
  2. Increase the difficulty of the game after the player reaches 200 points
  3. Increase the difficulty of the game after the player reaches 300 points
